This app connects the Adlar Castra or Aurora II heat pump to Homey Pro using
a wired Modbus RS485 gateway on your local network. No cloud connection is
needed for daily operation, which makes the integration fast, reliable and
independent of external services.

You get full control of your heat pump directly from Homey: adjust heating,
cooling and hot water setpoints, monitor temperatures, power consumption and
COP, and automate your system with Flow cards. A built-in local dashboard lets you monitor hundreds of individual Modbus
registers, configure your heat pump as an expert user including graphical
heating curve configuration, and set up detailed reports in Markdown format,
all within your home network.

Full technical documentation is available via the source code link on this
App Store page.
